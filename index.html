project description

The Russian war of aggression on Ukraine has catapulted the energy crisis to the center of social discourse. The energy crisis has been on everyone's lips for a good year now. Our electricity and gas bills are skyrocketing and we seem to be at the mercy of the system.

Daddy's favorite: Monsta Energy: a fairytale is a theater evening that resists this dependence. It questions the historical development of the concept of energy and navigates between the pursuit of efficiency and growth and the everyday abysses, the physical and psychological consequences. A visual-poetic negotiation of the questions of our time.

Handbook against [E]nergy

Hand-bound artist book. A fusion of art manifesto, queer-feminist-marxist theory, energy humanities, poetry, decolonial thinking and soviet constructivism:

"handbook against [E]nergy"


After an intensive confrontation with the academic history of the term Energy, we as a group felt the need for a shared foundation on which we could finally realize the manuscript and content of the theatre play, reflecting on what we found.
